01 — PRODUCT VISION

Stop tracking movement.
Start remembering a life.

Fitness apps optimise a number: pace, streak, VO₂max. But nobody wakes up dreaming of a bar chart. People move for meaning — to think, to grieve, to celebrate, to explore, to feel alive. Strivon turns the map of where you've moved into the story of who you've become.

🗺️

A living autobiography

Your journeys, memories, photos, voice notes and life events fuse into one growing map — a documentary you can replay.

💛

Meaning, not metrics

We optimise life outcomes, not step counts. Every walk connects to who you want to be at 40, 60, 80.

♾️

A moat made of memories

The more you live, the more it holds. You can export a workout. You can never export a decade of who you were.

02 — VALUE PROPOSITION

The one line

“Strava tells you how fast you ran. Strivon tells you who you were when you ran it — and hands that story to the people you love.”

For the user

A private, cinematic record of your life in motion — that motivates you emotionally and outlives you.

For families

Routes and voices preserved across generations. Walk your grandfather's Sunday loop and hear him tell it.

The wedge

Emotion is stickier than performance. We win on why people move — a space no incumbent owns.

02½ — PROOF

This isn't a deck. It's already live.

The wedge builds on a real, shipping product — Strivon today: a PWA with on-device sports-science intelligence, injury-risk safety, an AI coach and multi-race plans. The emotional layer layers straight onto this.

De-risked: the map, GPS, Supabase auth/data, Claude edge functions and a live subscription are already built. Strivon is the emotional expansion on top — not a from-scratch bet.
