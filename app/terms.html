Data we collect

We keep data collection to what the coaching service needs. Specifically:

  • Account — your email address, only if you create an account for cloud sync.
  • Training inputs — your goal, experience level, weeks, days per week, and optionally height and weight.
  • Activities — each run or walk: distance, duration, pace, route (GPS), heart rate and perceived effort where available.
  • Live location (GPS) — only while an activity is running, to record your route, distance and pace; it stops when you finish.
  • Readiness inputs — your daily sleep, soreness and energy answers, to adapt the session.
  • Imported workouts — any GPX/TCX or Apple Health / Health Connect data you choose to import.
  • Community — posts you choose to share (public and anonymous), plus a coarse ~40km region and, for route popularity, a fuzzed ~5km cell — never your exact start point.
  • AI coach — the messages you send and a summary of your training context, forwarded to our AI provider to generate replies (not used to train models).
  • Weather & air quality — an approximate location to fetch conditions for your area.
  • Payments — for Pro, billing is handled by our payment processor; we do not store your card details.
  • Diagnostics — minimal on-device error logs to fix crashes; never sold or used for advertising.

Most of this stays on your device by default and is only sent to our backend if you enable cloud sync. You can export or delete everything from Settings → Your data. Full detail — legal bases, retention and your rights — is in the Privacy Policy.
