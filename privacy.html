Privacy

Short version: your data is yours. Strivon runs on your device; nothing is sold, ever.

What we collect

Only what the app needs to coach you: your training inputs, activities, GPS routes during a session, and optional health imports. This lives on your device by default.

Location

GPS is used solely to record your route, distance and pace while a run or walk is active — including in the background if you allow it. Tracking stops when you finish. Routes never leave your device unless you enable cloud sync.

Health data

Files you import (Apple Health, Garmin, Strava, etc.) are processed locally to compute your fitness and injury metrics. We don't share health data with anyone.

Cloud sync (optional)

If you create an account, your data is stored on our backend (Supabase) so you can sync across devices, protected by row-level security. You can export or delete it any time from the app.

AI coach

Pro chat messages and a summary of your training context are sent to Anthropic's API to generate replies. They aren't used to train models and aren't stored beyond the request.

Your rights

Export a full backup or erase everything from Settings → Your data. No account is required to use the app, and none is required to delete your data. We don't sell data or run third-party ad trackers.
