How it's built

The Technology

Private by design, intelligent on your device, and built to feel effortless. Here’s the thinking — not the plumbing.

Principles

Built on four convictions

Private by design

Your journeys, memories and health data live on your device by default. Nothing is sold, and the social layer is anonymous by construction — you’re never the product.

Works offline

Capture a moment or track a route with no signal. Everything syncs quietly when you’re back online — the map is always there when you need it.

Intelligence on your device

The sports-science that keeps you injury-safe — pacing, load, readiness — runs on your phone in real time. Fast, and yours.

Effortless & cross-device

Start on your phone, glance at your watch, pick up on the web. Import from any wearable. It all stays in sync without you thinking about it.

The intelligence

A coach that actually knows you

Strivon’s intelligence isn’t one chatbot bolted on — it’s a set of specialists that quietly work together, and get more “you” every week.

🧠 Your coach

Warm, specific daily guidance grounded in your plan, runs, mood and the weather — never generic.

🧵 Your memory

Ask “what was I doing last spring?” and your own history answers.

🧭 Missions & routes

Fresh adventures and loops tuned to your distance, mood, scenery and the day.

📈 Patterns & wellbeing

It learns which places and rhythms lift you — and gently steers you toward them.

🔮 Your future self

See where your fitness and habits are heading, and test “what if?” before you commit.

🎬 Your story

Your year, narrated back to you as a film built from where you moved and how it felt.

The compounding advantage: everything you do teaches Strivon what moves you. The longer you’re here, the more it becomes irreplaceable — not because of lock-in, but because it holds your story.
