09 — MVP DEFINITION

The emotional wedge

Do not ship twelve pillars. Ship the smallest thing that makes someone say “I've never seen this” and can't delete a month later. Four features, built on Strivon's existing rails.

🗺️

Story Map

Capture a journey, pin a photo + voice + feeling. Replay your growing map.

⏳

Time Capsules

Record a message to future-you at a place; it resurfaces on return.

🎯

Daily Adventure

One AI mission a day. Weather/time/mood aware. LLM we already run.

🎖️

Explorer XP

Light RPG — XP, first titles, a streak that forgives. The habit engine.

Founder's honesty

The Emotional Engine, Digital Twin and Human Network are the moat — but they need scale and data you don't have yet. Build the four wedge features, let them generate the data, then those become defensible. Anyone who ships all twelve at once ships none of them well.
