03 — THE EXPERIENCE · 12 PILLARS

Twelve ways movement becomes meaning

Each pillar is a product in itself. Together they define a category no one else is playing in.

01
Memory

Life Story Map

Pin memories to places. Replay any era of your life.

“The run before my wedding.”
02
Time

Future-Self Messages

Leave a message at a place. Return later; the map hands you your past self.

“You recorded this training for your first marathon.”
03
Exploration

AI Adventure Generator

Daily missions tuned to your day: find a blue door, draw a star, reach water.

“Discover a street you've never walked.”
04
Wellbeing

Emotional Fitness Engine

It learns which places lift your mood — and sends you there.

“Riverside walks raise your mood 18%.”
05
Connection

Human Movement Network

Anonymous serendipity: paths you've crossed, kindred movers nearby.

“You crossed paths 24 times this year.”
06
Companion

Context-Aware AI Coach

Reads your sleep and mood. Empathy, not lectures.

“This is usually when your routine breaks.”
07
Play

City RPG System

The real world as an RPG — XP, classes, skill trees. Movement becomes adventure.

Bridge Walker · Sunrise Hunter · Forest Guardian
08
Legacy

Legacy Routes

Preserve a route with your voice. Your children walk it one day and hear you.

“Dad's favourite Sunday loop.”
09
Psychology

Personality Challenges

It learns your type — Explorer, Competitor, Reflector — and adapts every challenge.

Behavioural-psychology, not one-size-fits-all.
10
Discovery

Living Treasure Map

People drop hidden gems — viewpoints, quiet corners. The city, a treasure hunt.

Personalised by AI to your taste.
11
Prediction

Digital Twin

A model of your future fitness, burnout and mood. Test any “what if?”

See your projected futures, visually.
12
Purpose

Regret Minimiser

Anchor every move to what you want to still do at 80.

“0.001% closer to hiking Snowdon at 80.”
