Twelve ways movement becomes meaning — the pillars, the people who fall in love, the journeys that hook, the screens and the play.

03 — THE EXPERIENCE · 12 PILLARS

Twelve ways movement becomes meaning

Each pillar is a product in itself. The genius isn't building all twelve at once — it's the order (see §10). But together they define a category no one else is playing in.

01
Memory

Life Story Map

Pin memories, photos, voice notes and milestones to the places you moved. Replay any era of your life as a journey.

“The run before my wedding.”
02
Time

Future-Self Messages

Leave recordings, promises and predictions at a place. Return months later and the map surfaces your past self.

“You recorded this training for your first marathon.”
03
Exploration

AI Adventure Generator

Daily missions tuned to weather, time, mood and history: find a blue door, draw a star on the map, reach water.

“Discover a street you've never walked.”
04
Wellbeing

Emotional Fitness Engine

Log mood before/after. AI learns which places and routes lift you, and prescribes them.

“Riverside walks raise your mood 18%.”
05
Connection

Human Movement Network

Anonymous, privacy-first serendipity: paths you've unknowingly crossed, kindred explorers nearby.

“You crossed paths 24 times this year.”
06
Companion

Context-Aware AI Coach

Understands your calendar, workload, sleep and motivation cycles. Empathy, not lectures.

“This is usually when your routine breaks.”
07
Play

City RPG System

The real world becomes an RPG — XP, classes, titles, skill trees. Movement becomes adventure.

Bridge Walker · Sunrise Hunter · Forest Guardian
08
Legacy

Legacy Routes

Preserve a route forever with stories and voice. Your children walk it one day and hear you.

“Dad's favourite Sunday loop.”
09
Psychology

Personality Challenges

AI models your type — Explorer, Competitor, Reflector, Socialiser — and adapts every challenge.

Behavioural-psychology, not one-size-fits-all.
10
Discovery

Living Treasure Map

Users drop hidden viewpoints, quiet corners, photo spots. The city becomes a crowdsourced treasure hunt.

Personalised by AI to your taste.
11
Prediction

Digital Twin

A predictive model of you: future fitness, burnout risk, mood evolution. Test “what if I walk 20 min daily?”.

See your projected futures, visually.
12
Purpose

Regret Minimiser

Anchor every activity to what you want to still do at 80. Emotional motivation over numeric.

“0.001% closer to hiking Snowdon at 80.”

05 — USER JOURNEYS

Two moments that hook

The “I've never seen this” moment · Day 1
Open to a map, not a form. A single question: “Where did today take you?”
Walk a mission. AI: “Find a red door within 15 minutes.” You do. +40 XP, first badge.
Drop a memory. A photo + a 10-sec voice note pin to the map.
Leave a message to future-you. “Remember why you started.”
The map glows. Your first thread on the autobiography. You're in.
The moment you can never leave · Month 14
You return to a trailhead. The app surfaces a message you recorded a year ago.
Your past self speaks. You were anxious then. You made it. You cry a little.
Twin shows your arc. “14 months of Sunday walks → mood +22%, resting HR −6.”
Story engine narrates your year as a 90-second film.
You share one frame. A friend downloads. The loop compounds.
